The ultimate holiday destination

Jávea is the ultimate holiday destination for the whole family. Jávea is known for its beautiful, large palm-sand beach with a bustling boulevard (El Arenal), but you will also find small bays with crystal clear water here where you can enjoy swimming and snorkelling.

The old town of Jávea dates back to the Middle Ages. Here you can stroll through the narrow streets where you will find plenty of shops, restaurants, bars and even an authentic, covered fresh market "el Mercado".

The picturesque harbor "el Puerto" where fishing boats come in with their catch in the afternoon is also well worth a visit.

In Jávea there are many cozy and good restaurants. The offer is wide; authentic Spanish tapas bars hip beach bars where you can enjoy cocktails and very tasty food, but also chic restaurants for absolute fine dining.

You can enjoy the Costa Blanca all year round. There are fantastic walking and cycling routes and there are several golf courses in the area.
